SIMPLE FIGURE TRICK (OPEN TO CLOSE)


Created: 3.11 11:45 by Admin Sir
Views: 123175

Ye trick open/close figure nikalane ke liye hai....

IS TRICK ME 1441 KO JODATE HAI

Kisi bhi din Figure nikalane ke liye yesterday's ki jodi ko do bar likhakar usme 1441 jodna hai.... to jo figure banate hai wo otc aate hai.

Example:
Ab kalyan me friday ko 47 aayi thi

#Friday ko 47 Jodi banane par agle din -
(holiday ke karan monday)

=> 1441
=>+4747
=> ------
=> 5188

So open aya 5 aur close 1 aaya [Monday = 51]

#Tuesday ko 80 banane par wednesday ko-

=> 1441
=>+8080
=> ------
=> 9421

Open aya 9 [Wednesday = 90]

#Wednesday ko 90 banane par thursday ko-

=> 1441
=>+9090
=> ------
=> 0431

So open 4 aya aur close 1 [Thursday = 41]
